to give this sheet a name

You do not have to save a sheet, but may use it as a scratch sheet for experimenting.

Autorun is used for trending sheets and other custom sheets (like a RSS feed)

In other words, you are gathering counts or something over time.

Also, trending sheets must follow a certain template (except for custom sheets) where:

  • the first row is for headers, so you can identify what count is in what column
  • the second row is for scraper/api functions:
    typically, the first column of the second row is a url, username, or an video id (you may have multiple of these)
for example: 
     A                       |    B        |     C     |     D     |         E         |
 |---------------------------|-------------|-----------|-----------|-------------------|
1|                           |             |  Tweets   | fb shares |youtube subscribers|
2|http://some.com            |cleesmith2006|?tweets(a2)|?shares(a2)|?subscribers(B2)   |
3|Tue March 10, 2015 00:19:57|             | 2,100,233 | 700,000   | 1,999,888,777,332 |
4|Wed March 11, 2015 00:19:57|             | 2,100,234 | 700,001   | 1,999,888,777,333 |
